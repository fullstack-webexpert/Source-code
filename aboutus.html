    Bill Gaul
    Chief Executive Officer
    Bill graduated from West Point, and after serving in the Army as a helicopter pilot began a career in the high tech field. Initially as a product manager for a material science company, he always had aspirations of building his own company. Benefiting from various business contacts made in numerous companies, he transitioned to become an executive recruiter specializing in transitioning military to the corporate sector.

    After realizing the huge demand for his services, he started his first company in 1991, quickly growing it into a multi-million dollar operation. He sold that company in 1997 so that he could take advantage of the growing interest in the internet, starting one of the first employment websites, and becoming a frequently quoted expert for WSJ, Fortune, NPR and CNN in the field of employing transitioning military. He became a contributing author for two published books and numerous syndicated articles, selling that company in 2006 to a media conglomerate. After nearly 16 years in the employment field, Bill applied his knowledge of startup operations, technology, customer service, and software development into establishing Builders Update into the first national new home inventory database available to licensed real estate agents.
